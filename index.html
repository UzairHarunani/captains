SELECTION FOR AKPS HOUSE CAPTAINS

We are seeking passionate and dedicated House Captains to lead our School House Competition this year.
Candidates must be from Grade 6 and meet the following criteria:

  • Leadership Qualities: Ability to motivate and inspire their peers, take initiative. They should be role models, showcasing positive behavior, responsibility, and decision-making.
  • Academic and Behavioral Standards: Good academic behavior. Consistent attendance, a positive attitude toward learning, and following school rules.
  • Communication and Interpersonal Skills: Interact effectively with students, teachers, and the school community.
  • Commitment to House Activities: Participating in and promoting house events, activities, and competitions. They must be willing to dedicate time and effort to planning and organizing activities.
